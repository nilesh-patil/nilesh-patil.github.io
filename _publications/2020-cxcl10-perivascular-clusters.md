Imaging study (Center for Vaccine Biology, University of Rochester) characterizing CXCL10+ perivascular clusters as the spatial nucleation sites for Th1 cell entry and activation in inflamed skin tissue. Computational contribution: hyper-spectral microscopy reconstruction and 3D cluster identification pipelines that made the spatial pattern statistically resolvable across scans.
