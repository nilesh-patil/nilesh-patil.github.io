Cell Reports paper (with preprint at bioRxiv 2020.10.04.324525). CXCL10+ peripheral activation niches are spatially organized to couple preferred Th1 entry sites with the regions where antigen-presenting cell encounter is most efficient. Computational contribution: cluster identification, neighbor-statistics tooling, and 3D reconstruction infrastructure shared with the perivascular-clusters work.
